Program Overview

BridgeTech delivers an intensive 220-hour structured training program focused on equipping students with job-ready competencies through technical instruction, applied learning, and real-world exposure.

The curriculum combines technical skills training with professional soft skills development, hands-on industry projects, and comprehensive career readiness support, ensuring students are equipped not only with knowledge but with practical, industry-relevant competencies.
