Program Focus

Program Overview

The Naan Mudhalvan program is structured as a high-impact, short-duration training model, delivered over one to two weeks, focusing on equipping students with job-ready skills and career awareness.

As an implementing partner, YuMe Learning delivered intensive employability-focused training across multiple universities and colleges throughout Tamil Nadu, enabling students to gain exposure to high-demand technical and professional skills within a condensed time frame.
