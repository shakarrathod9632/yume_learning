About YuMe Learning

Your YuMe, Your Dream College is Over. Let’s Get You Hired.

At YuMe Learning, we help young graduates move from classroom theory to industry-ready skills. "YuMe" comes from the Japanese word for dream—and we're here to turn dreams into careers.

We focus on practical, hands-on training in emerging technologies, preparing you for jobs that exist today and will grow tomorrow. Our programs are industry-aligned and focused on outcomes, not just certificates.

Since we began, we've trained and placed over 300,000 students across 12 Indian states.

Rajesh Rathod

FOUNDER • YUME LEARNING • ENTREPRENEUR • WORKFORCE ARCHITECT

Rajesh Rathod is a serial entrepreneur and lifelong learner who has spent over two decades building platforms that help young people transition from education to employment. As the founder of Yume Learning, he focuses on bridging the gap between emerging technologies and accessible, job-linked training for graduates.

He has consistently worked in sunrise sectors — launching mobile phones in 1996, promoting internet payments in 2000, entering the skilling space in 2010, and most recently, popularizing AI learning since 2024. Across 12 Indian states, his companies have directly trained more than 5 lakh learners, many from underserved backgrounds.

Before founding Yume, Rajesh co-founded Paradigm Consulting and Ants & Partners, and mentored SAFAL Trust, leading skilling programs in collaboration with government bodies and civil society.

He has contributed to large-scale initiatives such as STAR, Skill India, BridgeTech, Naan Mudhalvan, and Kalike Jothege Kaushalya (KJK) — all focused on practical, outcome-driven education.

A product of IIM Ahmedabad, with a degree in Physics from Mumbai University, Rajesh’s early career in advertising & telecom shaped his sharp professional instincts. He draws inspiration from Bill Bernbach’s honest creativity and Alfred E. Neuman’s playful irreverence — a mix that reflects in both his work and worldview.

Outside of work, Rajesh enjoys traveling through India’s small towns, watching oddball foreign films, listening to rock and folk music, and getting lost on treks — always curious, always moving forward.

Dr. Rumi Sikdar

CO-FOUNDER • ANTS SKILL VARSITY & YUME LEARNING • SOCIAL ENTREPRENEUR • SKILLING LEADER

Dr. Rumi Sikdar is a serial entrepreneur and changemaker who has worked at the intersection of skills, livelihoods, and social impact for over two decades. As Co-Founder of Yume Learning and Chief Functionary of SAFAL Trust, she has shaped inclusive skilling ecosystems across India, especially for women, youth, and underserved communities.

Since 2005, she has built operations that trained more than 2 lakh learners across India—through government partnerships, CSR programs, and grassroots initiatives. Her work includes major projects under NSQF, NULM, PMKVY, EGMM, and Higher Education Programs such as Naan Mudhalvan, KJK, Microsoft Education, and NASSCOM Future Skills Prime.

She holds a PhD on NSQF impact and has contributed to curriculum reform, school-based skill programs, Higher Education skilling pathways, and community entrepreneurship. Her journey reflects resilience—building teams of 400+, scaling operations with technology, and emerging as one of India’s early women leaders merging social impact with enterprise.

Her professional experience includes impactful roles at TARU, CEE, YUVA, and Dr. Reddy’s Foundation, working across environment, disability, and youth development. She is an alumna of IIM Bangalore, a UNCTAD-certified Empretec entrepreneur, and a strong advocate for women-led nano enterprises.

Beyond work, Rumi is an avid traveler (33 countries), an enthusiastic gardener, and a design lover with deep passion for interiors and landscaping. She has been recognized with honors like Social Entrepreneur of Karnataka (2017), Women Entrepreneur of the Year (2018), and featured in CEO Magazine (2023).

Dr. Ravi Shankar

Dr. Ravishankar is a global thought leader in framing accreditation and certification standards for Data Science & AI-ML. He specializes in IR4.0 technologies and its application to solve business problems across the Education, Pharma, Insurance and IT sectors. He crafts strategic learning journeys and delivers AI-ML programs for CXOs in Healthcare, BFSI and Retail/CPG. Dr. Ravi Shankar is also an entrepreneur, mentor & investor in the AgFood Tech and rural impact startup ecosystems.

Currently a Visiting Professor at leading business institutes in India and abroad including the prestigious ISB and Singapore Management University. He specializes in teaching Strategic AI Leadership, AI Business Adoption, Computer Vision and Deep Learning. He also consults and drives Data Science–driven Digital Transformation initiatives at Fortune 1000 companies. Ravi has over three decades of expertise at the intersection of Agricultural Policy & Data Analytics.

He is actively involved in transforming the sugarcane industry from a net-zero perspective. He is also the co-founder of Gram Paathshala, Bharat Mandi and NextWave Tech. His past work includes leadership roles at ICAR, AMEX, Blackstone, Dell, GE Healthcare and PwC, across analytics-led consulting in Agriculture, BFSI, Healthcare, CPG-Retail and Technology in the Indo-US corridor. He also works on scaling FPOs/FPCs and driving innovation & technology adoption across Indian Ag-Food value systems.

A seasoned AgTech mentor associated with multiple accelerators & incubators including UASB, C-CAMP, Ah Ventures, FAAD Network, A-Idea NAARM (AgriUdaan), AgFoodTech360, FKCCI Startups, TiE Bangalore and the Karnataka Digital Economy Mission (KDEM). He contributes to national agricultural research and policy as an Advisory Board Member at NDRI, IIHR, MoFPI, ICRISAT, CIMMYT, ITC, JUAIV and others. Most recently, he was selected as an Indian Administrative Fellow (IAF) with the Union Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment.
