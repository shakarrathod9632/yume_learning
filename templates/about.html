About Us

About YuMe Learning

Your YuMe, Your Dream
College is Over. Let’s Get You Hired.

At YuMe Learning, we help young graduates move from classroom theory to industry-ready skills. "YuMe" comes from the Japanese word for dream—and we're here to turn dreams into careers.

We focus on practical, hands-on training in emerging technologies, preparing you for jobs that exist today and will grow tomorrow. Our programs are industry-aligned and focused on outcomes, not just certificates.

Since we began, we've trained and placed over 300,000 students across 12 Indian states.

Who We Are

Our leadership includes IIM alumni and professionals with decades of experience in corporate strategy, education, and social impact. We've worked with governments, built training systems, and placed thousands of graduates.

At YuMe, we don't just run courses. We help you build your edge, land your first job, and start your real journey.

About The Founder

Rajesh Rathod

FOUNDER • YUME LEARNING • ENTREPRENEUR • WORKFORCE ARCHITECT

Rajesh Rathod is a serial entrepreneur and lifelong learner who has spent over two decades building platforms that help young people transition from education to employment. As the founder of Yume Learning, he focuses on bridging the gap between emerging technologies and accessible, job-linked training for graduates.

He has consistently worked in sunrise sectors — launching mobile phones in 1996, promoting internet payments in 2000, entering the skilling space in 2010, and most recently, popularizing AI learning since 2024.

Before founding Yume, Rajesh co-founded Paradigm Consulting and Ants & Partners, and mentored SAFAL Trust, leading skilling programs with government bodies.

He has contributed to programs like STAR, Skill India, BridgeTech, Naan Mudhalvan, and Kalike Jothege Kaushalya (KJK).

An IIM Ahmedabad alumnus with a Physics degree from Mumbai University, Rajesh blends strategy, creativity, and execution.

About The Co-Founder

Dr. Rumi Sikdar

CO-FOUNDER • ANTS SKILL VARSITY & YUME LEARNING • SOCIAL ENTREPRENEUR • SKILLING LEADER

Dr. Rumi Sikdar is a serial entrepreneur and changemaker who has worked at the intersection of skills, livelihoods, and social impact for over two decades. As Co-Founder of Yume Learning and Chief Functionary of SAFAL Trust, she has shaped inclusive skilling ecosystems across India, especially for women, youth, and underserved communities.

Since 2005, she has built operations that trained more than 2 lakh learners across India—through government partnerships, CSR programs, and grassroots initiatives. Her work includes major projects under NSQF, NULM, PMKVY, EGMM, and Higher Education Programs such as Naan Mudhalvan, KJK, Microsoft Education, and NASSCOM Future Skills Prime.

She holds a PhD on NSQF impact and has contributed to curriculum reform, school-based skill programs, Higher Education skilling pathways, and community entrepreneurship. Her journey reflects resilience—building teams of 400+, scaling operations with technology, and emerging as one of India's early women leaders merging social impact with enterprise.

Her professional experience includes impactful roles at TARU, CEE, YUVA, and Dr. Reddy's Foundation, working across environment, disability, and youth development. She is an alumna of IIM Bangalore, a UNCTAD-certified Empretec entrepreneur, and a strong advocate for women-led nano enterprises.

Beyond work, Rumi is an avid traveler (33 countries), an enthusiastic gardener, and a design lover with deep passion for interiors and landscaping. She has been recognized with honors like Social Entrepreneur of Karnataka (2017), Women Entrepreneur of the Year (2018), and featured in CEO Magazine (2023).
