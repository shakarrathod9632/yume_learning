Program Overview

The KJK Program delivers industry-relevant skills through a 50-hour hybrid learning model, combining classroom instruction with hands-on practical exposure across high-demand technology domains.

Students earn industry-recognized certifications from leading organizations including Adobe, Microsoft, and Tally, strengthening their credentials and employability while bridging the gap between academic learning and industry expectations.
